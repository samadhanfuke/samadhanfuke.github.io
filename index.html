About Me

Senior Software & Platform Engineer with 4.8+ years of hands-on experience designing, building, and operating Linux-based infrastructure and cloud-native platforms across edge, on-prem, and hybrid cloud environments.

I specialize in platform engineering, DevOps automation, and containerized systems, with strong expertise in Python and Bash–driven automation, Linux system engineering, Kubernetes/OpenShift, CI/CD pipelines, and Infrastructure as Code. My background includes delivering production-grade Edge AI and IoT platforms spanning bare metal Intel NUCs and Xeon servers, GPU-enabled systems, virtualized environments (VMware, Hyper-V, KVM/QEMU, Synology), and public cloud platforms.

Most recently, I have joined as a Senior Cloud / OpenShift Platform Engineer through a partner organization, currently in the onboarding and enablement phase for enterprise OpenShift and cloud platform environments supporting a Nokia client engagement.

Previously, I spent nearly 4.8 years at Futops Technologies India Pvt. Ltd., where I played a key role in building and operating large-scale Edge AI and cloud-connected platforms for industrial, retail, and smart-city use cases. My responsibilities included CI/CD and GitOps engineering, automated OS provisioning using FAI and PXE, Kubernetes and OpenShift deployments, hybrid edge–cloud architectures, and production L3/L4 operational support.

I have extensive experience designing and maintaining CI/CD pipelines using GitHub Actions, Jenkins, and GitLab CI; implementing GitOps workflows with ArgoCD; and managing infrastructure using Terraform, Ansible, Helm, and CloudFormation. I have led large-scale Linux provisioning initiatives, automating the deployment of 1000+ systems while significantly reducing provisioning and release times.

From an SRE and operations perspective, I have built and operated observability stacks using Prometheus, Grafana, Netdata, Zabbix, and InfluxDB, enabling proactive monitoring, alerting, and incident response across distributed environments. I have also supported system upgrades, DR drills, security hardening, and production troubleshooting.

I have worked closely with cross-functional teams and Japanese enterprise clients, contributing across the full platform lifecycle from architecture and automation to deployment, monitoring, and long-term system stability. I value engineering discipline, clarity, and maintainability, and I focus on building platforms that are reliable, observable, and upgrade-safe in real production environments.
